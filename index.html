TROLL FIGHT

Oh no! Your party has come across a MONSTROUS TROLL in the woods! If you don’t slay him, you won’t be able to pass through.

You know, as does every adventurer, that trolls have CORROSIVE BLOOD, and therefore, if you damage him past the point of death, your entire party will face CERTAIN DEATH.

Each party member does a different amount of damage. Choose which fighter to attack with, and see if you can defeat this threat!

You will not know the amount of damage each party member deals until you click them. This value will change every time you play the game, so player beware.
